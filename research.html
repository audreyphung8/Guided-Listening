User Research - Affinity Diagram
1. We interviewed 10 people about their experience as full-time college students.
2. Based on the interviews conducted, each team member took notes on their participants and recorded their responses.
3. We grouped common themes that we found across all the notes.

External stress factors include family expectations, balancing academics with extracurriculars, and seeking professional opportunities.

Students experience the most stress when they have upcoming deadlines or exams.

Participants want an Ad-free experience and a UI that has calm colors and friendly cartoon visuals.

A trusted mental-health focused app should keep all personal data secured and feedback anonymous.

Students relieve stress by listening to music, spend time with friends and family, and practice meditation.

Participants listen to music while studying, working out, and driving. The type of music depends on the activity and their current mood.

4. Used the insights and themes that we gathered to create our persona, Chloe Nguyen!
